ÖCS 2022 proposal accepted🎓

Posted on January 20, 2022 by Eurac Research ‐  1 min read

We’re delighted to share that our proposal to present a research poster on Zeit.shift at the 7th conference of the Austrian Citizen Science network has been accepted!

The presentation, entitled "„Digital in gesterns Zukunft“: Kulturelles Texterbe Gesamttirols bewahren – erschließen – vermitteln", will be delivered by Johanna Walcher (ULB Tirol) at the end of June 2022 in Dornbirn, Austria.

The full conference programme is available at: https://www.oesterreichische-citizen-science-konferenz-2022.com/konferenzablauf

The poster programme is available at: https://www.oesterreichische-citizen-science-konferenz-2022.com/poster
